# Final Content Rights Notes

## Current Audit

- No RCMP, OPP, TPS, YRP, or other police-service logo is intentionally included.
- No official government badge or coat of arms is intentionally included.
- Branding uses generic OPAi, maple leaf, shield, and police-officer imagery.
- Application code uses open-source dependencies listed in `package.json` and `pnpm-lock.yaml`.
- App Store and Google Play badges, if used in marketing, must follow their brand guidelines.

## Required Owner Confirmation

Before public submission, the project owner must confirm ownership or an appropriate license for
the app icon, splash image, brand banner, PTSD emblem, store screenshots, fonts, and any externally
created imagery. Generic appearance does not prove asset ownership.

Do not claim rights to official police or government marks without written authorization. Answer
the App Store content-rights question based on the assets in the actual uploaded binary and the
owner's provenance records. If all submitted assets are original or properly licensed, record that
confirmation with the release evidence.
