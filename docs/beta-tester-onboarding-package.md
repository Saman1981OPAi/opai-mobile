# Beta Tester Onboarding Package

## Welcome Message

Welcome to the OPAi Police internal beta. This is a testing/pre-launch local prototype intended to
evaluate usability, workflow, local reminders, and iOS release readiness.

## Tester Rules

- Use demo data only.
- Do not enter real police records, confidential information, sensitive personal information,
  statements, official documents, or operational details.
- Do not upload or reference real evidence.
- Do not rely on mock AI or translation responses.
- Do not treat reminders as official obligations, policy, supervision, or court/training records.
- Do not use the app during an emergency or as a replacement for approved systems.

## What to Test

- Mock authentication, consent, and sign-out
- Home Dashboard and Start My Shift
- New Incident local drafts
- Translation and OPAi Assistant mock workflows
- Calendar, Court, Training, Requalification, and Follow-Ups
- Notes & Files metadata placeholders
- Settings, Privacy, Terms, and disclaimers
- Reset Demo Data and Clear Local Data
- Local notification permission, scheduling, and cancellation

## Reporting Issues

Use TestFlight feedback when available or email support@opaiapp.com. Include device model, iOS
version, build number, screen/module, steps to reproduce, expected result, actual result, and a safe
screenshot when possible. Remove all real operational or personal information before sending.
