# Privacy Policy Draft

OPAi is currently in testing/pre-launch.

In this prototype:

- Data is stored locally on the user's device.
- The app uses local mock authentication.
- The app does not connect to a production backend.
- The app does not call OpenAI.
- The app does not upload files.
- The app does not sync with police systems.
- The app does not use advertising.
- The app does not use third-party analytics.

Users should not enter real police records, evidence, confidential information, sensitive personal information, real statements, or official documents into this prototype.

Future production versions may use secure backend services, encrypted storage, authentication, audit logging, AI services, and optional integrations.
