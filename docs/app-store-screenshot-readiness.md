# App Store Screenshot Readiness

## Current Status

Sprint 015 improves screenshot readiness by tightening visual states, validation feedback, empty states, and accessibility labels. The app remains a local/offline testing prototype and should be shown as pre-launch where appropriate.

## Recommended iOS Screenshot Set

- Home Dashboard
- Start My Shift
- New Incident
- AI Assistant
- Translation
- Calendar or Notes & Files

## Screenshot Requirements

- Use iOS-first screenshots.
- Keep Android screenshots and Google Play assets paused until the D-U-N-S Number is received.
- Avoid real police records, real evidence, real names, confidential information, or sensitive personal data.
- Keep "testing/pre-launch" language visible where appropriate.
- Keep the OPAi dark blue/black theme, police blue accents, PTSD awareness accent `#7FFFD4`, and maple leaf/shield branding.
- Do not use official police service logos, government badges, RCMP, OPP, TPS, YRP, or other protected insignia.

## Pre-Submission Review

- Confirm app name and subtitle are consistent with App Store metadata.
- Confirm screenshots do not imply official government endorsement.
- Confirm AI and PTSD disclaimers remain accessible in the app.
- Confirm Privacy Policy and Terms of Use screens are reachable from Settings.
- Confirm the app does not expose developer Apple ID information in the UI.
