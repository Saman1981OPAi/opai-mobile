# Beta Tester Instructions

## What To Test

- Welcome, Sign In, and Create Account mock flow.
- Consent flow.
- Home Dashboard.
- Start My Shift.
- New Incident.
- Translation.
- OPAi Assistant.
- Calendar.
- Court.
- Training and requalification.
- Follow-Ups.
- Notes & Files.
- Settings.
- Privacy, Terms, and disclaimers.
- Reset Demo Data.
- Clear Local Data.
- Local notifications.

## Tester Rules

- Use demo/mock data only.
- Do not enter real police records.
- Do not enter real personal information.
- Do not upload or reference real evidence.
- Do not rely on AI mock content.
- Do not treat reminders as official obligations.
- Report bugs, confusing screens, broken flows, spelling mistakes, layout problems, or missing disclaimers.

## What To Include In Feedback

- Device model.
- iOS version.
- App version/build.
- Screen or module.
- Steps to reproduce.
- Expected result.
- Actual result.
- Screenshot or video if available.
- Severity: Critical, High, Medium, or Low.
