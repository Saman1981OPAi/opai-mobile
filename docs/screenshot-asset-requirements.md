## Screenshot Review Notes

- iPhone final screenshots are organized under `store-assets/ios-6.9-inch-final` and `store-assets/ios-6.5-inch-final`.
- iPad final screenshots are organized under `store-assets/ipad-13-inch-final`.
- The final iPhone 6.9-inch and iPad 13-inch assets use accepted App Store dimensions.
- Assets show OPAi-created maple leaf/shield-style branding and do not require Android release preparation.
- Final screenshots should be rechecked against the exact iOS build submitted to Apple.
- Do not use RCMP, OPP, TPS, YRP, government badges, or official police-service insignia in screenshots.
- Screenshots should not imply the app is fully launched if it remains testing/pre-launch.

## Android Status

Android screenshot and Google Play production-release preparation are paused until the D-U-N-S Number for Ebrahimi Holdings is received. Do not delete Android configuration or break Android compatibility.
