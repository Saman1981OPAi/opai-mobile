# App Store Review Readiness

OPAi Police is being prepared as an iOS-first testing/pre-launch app.

## Current Review Position

- iOS is the active launch priority.
- Android and Google Play release remain paused pending the D-U-N-S Number for Ebrahimi Holdings.
- The app is clearly marked as testing/pre-launch.
- The app does not claim official police service or government affiliation.
- The app uses generic maple leaf/shield branding and avoids official police logos, government badges, RCMP marks, OPP marks, TPS marks, YRP marks, and other protected insignia.

## Current Technical Boundaries

- No production backend integration.
- No OpenAI API integration.
- No production database integration.
- No real file upload or cloud storage.
- No payment or subscription code.
- No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
- No police-service integrations.
- No production secrets.

## Data and Privacy

Prototype data is local/offline data only. Users are warned not to enter real police records, evidence, statements, confidential information, sensitive personal information, or official documents.

## Legal and Support Readiness

The app includes local drafts for Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and required disclaimers. Support, privacy, security, and legal contact emails are displayed in Settings.

Privacy Policy and Terms URLs should be verified before final App Store submission:

- https://opaiapp.com/privacy
- https://opaiapp.com/terms
