# Build 25 App Privacy Impact

Build 25 introduces user-initiated transmission of prompt text, report facts, voice recordings, selected images, and selected documents to the OPAi backend and OpenAI. These categories and purposes must be reflected accurately in App Store Connect App Privacy answers before public submission.

The app adds no analytics, advertising, tracking, background location, social SDKs, payment processing, or police-system integration. Provider storage is disabled.
