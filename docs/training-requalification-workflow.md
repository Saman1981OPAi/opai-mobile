# Training and Requalification Workflow

## Purpose

The Training workflow tracks local prototype training events and requalification reminders.

## Training Events

Fields:

- Title
- Category
- Date
- Time
- Location
- Instructor or unit
- Notes
- Reminder enabled
- Reminder lead time
- Status: upcoming, completed, cancelled

Categories:

- Firearms
- Use of Force
- CEW
- CPR / First Aid
- Scenario Training
- Policy Training
- Other

## Requalification Reminders

Fields:

- Title
- Category
- Due date
- Expiry date
- Notes
- Reminder enabled
- Reminder lead time
- Status

The app calculates requalification status locally:

- Valid: more than 30 days away
- Due soon: due within 30 days
- Overdue: due date has passed
- Completed: manually marked complete

## Safety Notice

Training and requalification reminders are supportive productivity aids only. Users must verify mandatory training, qualification status, policy obligations, and deadlines through authorized systems and supervisors.
