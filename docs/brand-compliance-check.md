# Brand Compliance Check

## OPAi Brand Requirements

- Dark blue/black interface.
- Police blue interaction accents.
- PTSD awareness accent color `#7FFFD4`.
- Maple leaf/shield-inspired branding.
- Professional, minimal, premium visual tone.
- Clear testing/pre-launch positioning.

## Protected Insignia Rules

The app must not use official police service logos or protected government insignia, including:

- RCMP
- OPP
- TPS
- YRP
- Other official police-service crests, seals, or badges
- Government agency badges or protected marks

## Sprint 015 Result

Sprint 015 preserved the existing OPAi brand system and did not add official police or government insignia. The work was limited to UI polish, accessibility labels, confirmation dialogs, empty states, validation messages, and documentation.

## Ongoing Review

Before each App Store submission, verify screenshots, app icons, in-app logos, marketing copy, and metadata do not imply official endorsement or affiliation with any police service or government body.
