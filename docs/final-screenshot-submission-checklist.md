# Final Screenshot Submission Checklist

## Accepted Sizes to Confirm in App Store Connect

### iPhone 6.9-inch

- 1260 x 2736 portrait or 2736 x 1260 landscape
- 1290 x 2796 portrait or 2796 x 1290 landscape
- 1320 x 2868 portrait or 2868 x 1320 landscape

### iPad

- 2064 x 2752 portrait or 2752 x 2064 landscape
- 2048 x 2732 portrait or 2732 x 2048 landscape

## Checklist

- [ ] Confirm current App Store Connect size requirements immediately before upload.
- [ ] Use PNG, JPG, or JPEG accepted by App Store Connect.
- [ ] Match the actual uploaded app functionality and current UI.
- [ ] Put the strongest three screenshots first.
- [ ] Exclude official police or government insignia.
- [ ] Exclude real personal data, police records, evidence, statements, and documents.
- [ ] Confirm all visual assets are original or licensed.
- [ ] Avoid misleading AI, legal, medical, translation, and operational claims.
- [ ] Keep text inside App Store crop and safe areas.
- [ ] Check iPhone and iPad layouts for overlap, clipping, and illegible text.
- [ ] Use consistent OPAi dark blue/black, police blue, and `#7FFFD4` awareness accents.
- [ ] Label testing or mock workflows where the screenshot could imply production functionality.

Recommended first iPhone screens: Home Dashboard, Start My Shift, then New Incident or OPAi
Assistant. Recommended first iPad screens: Dashboard overview, Calendar/Court/Training, then OPAi
Assistant or Notes & Files.

The existing screenshot designs are not certified as current until captured or compared against
the final build `21` binary on physical iPhone and iPad devices.
