# Calendar Workflow

## Purpose

The Calendar workflow lets a user manage local prototype schedule items across court, training, requalification, follow-up, meeting, shift, and general categories.

## Current Sprint 009 Behavior

- Add, edit, delete, complete, reopen, and cancel local calendar items.
- Filter items by type.
- Store title, type, date, time, location, notes, status, reminder setting, and reminder lead time.
- Schedule local-only reminders when enabled.
- Cancel local reminders when an item is completed, cancelled, deleted, or reminder-disabled.

## Explicit Non-Scope

- No Apple Calendar sync.
- No Google Calendar sync.
- No Outlook sync.
- No cloud sync.
- No backend calendar API.
- No automatic court or police-service schedule import.

## Safety Notice

Calendar items are productivity aids only. Users must verify official dates, locations, and obligations through authorized systems.
