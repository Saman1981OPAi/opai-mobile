## Source Notes

- The CRTC describes Canadian 9-1-1 service and its connection to police, fire, and paramedic dispatch. Availability language remains qualified because service details vary by jurisdiction and telephone service.
- 9-8-8 confirms toll-free call and text support across Canada, 24/7/365.
- 211 Canada confirms free and confidential access in most of Canada, usually 24 hours a day and in more than 150 languages. The app preserves the provider's qualification that local availability may vary.
- Boots on the Ground confirms the 24/7 number, Ontario-based scope, first-responder audience, and anonymous peer-support role. The app does not represent the service as therapy.
- CMHA provides a national branch/region finder. No single availability claim is attached because branch services and hours vary.
- YSSN's current 310-COPE page confirms its number, 24/7 phone/text availability, and the York Region, South Simcoe, and North York coverage displayed in the app. Its notice that calls may be recorded for training is also shown in the resource card.
