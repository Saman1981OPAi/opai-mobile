# Sprint 012: AI Assistant Mock Workflow and Safety Layer

Sprint 012 expands the OPAi Assistant into a structured local prototype. It adds categories, category-specific prompt chips, suggested actions, a chat-style prompt surface, mock responses, local AI history, safety notices, and simple local placeholders for saving AI output to notes or attaching it to an incident draft.

This sprint remains mock/local only. It does not add OpenAI API calls, model calls, backend APIs, production databases, cloud sync, police-service integrations, legal databases, payment or subscription code, production secrets, analytics, advertising SDKs, or Android production release work.

## Prototype Features

- AI categories for General Support, Shift Readiness Helper, Report Review, Incident Note Organizer, Follow-Up Suggestions, Court Reminder Helper, Calendar Assistant, Training / Requalification Helper, Translation Support, Criminal Code / Legal Reference Placeholder, Policy Reminder Placeholder, PTSD / Stress Support, and Wellness Check-In.
- Category-specific prompt chips for wellness check-ins, PTSD/stress support, incident note organization, court reminders, training/requalification reminders, shift readiness, policy placeholders, and general support.
- Suggested action cards for report review, incident summaries, follow-ups, court preparation, notes organization, training deadlines, calendar reminders, Criminal Code placeholder search, policy placeholder search, and PTSD awareness messaging.
- Local prompt input and mock response card.
- Local AI history with category filtering, individual delete, and clear-all confirmation.
- Local history preference and last-selected category preference.
- Incident draft attachment placeholder.
- Save mock response to local notes placeholder.

## Safety Boundaries

- Legal reference and policy features are placeholders only.
- Wellness/PTSD content is supportive and educational only.
- The OPAi Assistant prototype does not provide legal advice, medical advice, operational direction, or emergency support. Users must follow service policy, law, training, and supervisor direction.
- Mock responses never claim legal accuracy, official report completion, medical guidance, therapy, diagnosis, or crisis intervention.
- Users are warned not to enter real police records, confidential information, sensitive personal information, real statements, evidence, or official documents.

## Validation Scope

The Sprint 012 review should confirm typecheck, iOS export, Android compatibility export, local launch smoke test, no network/OpenAI/backend/database calls, no secrets, and that existing Sprint 003-011 workflows still open after mock sign-in.

Android compatibility remains preserved, but Google Play and Android production release activity remain paused until the D-U-N-S Number for Ebrahimi Holdings is received.
