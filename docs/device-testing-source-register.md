## Governance

- Recheck federal approved-device orders regularly.
- Recheck approved-instrument lists regularly.
- Recheck manufacturer guides when model or software revisions change.
- Recheck provincial law and regulations.
- Mark outdated or unsupported guides unavailable until reviewed.
- Never silently retain outdated procedures.
